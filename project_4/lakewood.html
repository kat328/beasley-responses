Lakewood Brewing Co. | Dallas, TX

www. lakewoodbrewing.com

Lakewood Brewing was founded in 2011 by a gentleman who immigrated to the US from Belgium when he was 7 years old. He developed an appreciation for Belgian-style beer from his trips to Belgium over the years. Lakewood strives to create high-quality beers and stay true to the art and precision of craft brewing.

The Lakewood Brewing Taproom & Beer Garden is open Wednesday through Sunday, and they offer tours on Saturdays and Sundays.
