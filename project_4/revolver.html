Revolver Brewing | Granbury, TX

Revolver Brewing is a Texas family owned business in Granbury, TX. They say that they don't take themselves too seriously, but they take extreme pride in their craft. They brew each beer with water from their Trinity Aquifer well and use seasonal and local ingredients. Their Master Brewer previously worked as a Senior Brewmaster for Samuel Adams and has an extreme love of the craft.

Revolver Brewing is open for tours each Saturday, and the cost is $10/person. Each person admitted who is 21+ will receive a free pint glass and get to sample 3 beers. The brewery often hosts musical acts and brings in local food providers during the tour, so there is plenty to do for all members of the family.
