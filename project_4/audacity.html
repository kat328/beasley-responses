Audacity Brew House | Denton, TX

Audacity Brew House is located in the heart of Denton. They are quickly becoming the most recognizable breweries in Denton. Audacity has a taproom that is open 6 days each week, and they often have their brand new beers on tap for visitors to try before they go out for distribution.

The Audacity Brew House taproom is open Tuesday through Sunday, and they often have live music, open mic nights, and yoga classes. Visit their website to learn more about the beers and events available at Audacity.
