Armadillo Ale Works | Denton, TX

Armadillo Ale Works began in 2010 with some recipes and a business plan. Its founders knew that Denton needed its own craft brewery, so they raised over $34,000 it its Kickstarter campaign. Since then, they won a gold medal at the Great American Beer Festival in 2014. Deep Ellum Brewing Company has helped Armadillo Ale Works get its start, and Armadillo Ale works plans to open their own facility in Denton very soon.
