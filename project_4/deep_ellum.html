Deep Ellum Brewing Co. | Dallas, TX

Deep Ellum Brewing Co. is located in the heart of Deep Ellum. The folks at Deep Ellum are committed to making great beer because they like to drink great beer. They are also fierce supporters of local businesses and breweries. In fact, they supported Denton's Armadillo Ale Works as the up-and-coming brewery built their business and secured their own location in Denton.

The Deep Ellum Taproom is open from 3pm to 10pm Monday-Friday and 12pm to 10pm on Saturdays. They also offer an open house each Saturday from 12pm to 3pm.
