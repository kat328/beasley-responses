Rahr & Sons Brewing Co.

Rahr & Sons has over 10 years of brewing tradition. They produce over 20,000 barrels a year and have won over 50 nationally-recognized awards, including a Silver Medal at the 2012 Great American Beer Festival, a Bronze Medal at the 2008 World Beer Cup, and was the 2009 National Grand Champion at the United States Beer Tasting Championship.

Rahr offers tours and tastings each Wednesday from 5pm to 7:30pm and Saturday from 1pm to 3pm. Admission is $10, and visitors receive a free pint glass and up to 3 free beers.
